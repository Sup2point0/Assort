# Tekk-Knight

An archetype of LIGHT Celestial Warrior monsters, inspired by the [Mekk-Knight](https://yugipedia.com/wiki/Mekk-Knight) archetype.

The Hi-Tekk-Knight subarchetype are the Extra Deck monsters, while the [Ark-Knight](Ark-Knight.md) subarchetype features the 4 strongest monsters of each Summoning technique.
